This tool was developed in partnership with Aberystwyth University under the GEOM programme and part-funded by the European Regional Development Fund through the Welsh Government. The GEOM Programme, is a major EU backed initiative to help companies in Wales to exploit the fast-growing spatial intelligence market and is being led by Aberystwyth University in partnership with QinetiQ. The tool is a derivative work of ODK Collect by the Open Data Kit project. Please see the Licensing section for all derivative licenses.
